| Capital Job Initiation (JI)   |
|-------------------------------|

| Introduction   |
|----------------|

| The Job Initiation Form is a working document for managing and directing a capital project.  It is the focal point at which information relating to the project is gathered for approval by Management and serves to remind all of the project scope.  A clear understanding of the project ensures that the major needs are identified and incorporated into the planning of the project.   |
|----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| The primary use of the document is to act as a base document against which approval to advance can be given, progress can be assessed, and changes can be made as needed.                                                                                                                                                                                                                    |
| Parts of the JI Form might need to be amended as the project progresses.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     |

| Process Flow   |
|----------------|

| To create a Job number                                        |
|---------------------------------------------------------------|
| Complete JI Form tab - section 1                              |
| Submit JI Form to Site Manager/Utility Coordinator for review |
| Submit JI Form to DU President/CEO for approval               |

| Prior to Construction                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        |
|----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| Complete JI Form tab - section 1 & 2                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         |
| Complete Cash Flow tab                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       |
| Submit JI Form and Cash Flow to DU President/CEO for approval                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                |
| Acquisition or construction of new facilities shall be done in accordance with the Army and Management Committee’s approved capital plan, or modifications to the contracts.  Prior to approving the acquisition or construction of a new asset, management shall be presented with an estimate of the full cost to purchase or construct the new asset.  Upon determination of approval, management and/or the Management Committee will ensure resources are adequate to meet these needs. |
| Upon approval, signed documents should be forwarded to Accounting at USA.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    |
| Job #s will be created in NAV Job System and provided via email to requesting party.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         |
| Copies of all signed contracts are provided to Accounting and filed in the job file (which will be incorporated into the CPR (Continuing Property Record)).                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  |
| A copy of all project plans and specifications are provided to accounting and kept in the job file.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          |

| All change orders require approval by management.                                        |
|------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| Updates/changes are provided to Accounting regularly.                                    |
| Notify Accounting when the project is scheduled to be completed and placed into service. |

| Capital Job Completion (JC)   |
|-------------------------------|

| Introduction   |
|----------------|

| The Job Completion Form is a trigger document for alerting all to the acquisition of and begin the final capitalization of a capital project.  It is the trigger point at which information relating to the finalization of the project is reviewed by Management and serves to  update and capsulate all of the information for the Property Record.  A clear understanding of what was acquired throughout the project ensures that the acquisitions are identified and incorporated into the final costs and recording of the fixed asset.   |
|-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| The primary use of the document is to act as a base document against which approval to close can be given, final paperwork can be requested, and the job file can be prepared for final review prior to close.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  |

Upon determination of approval, management and/or the Management Committee will ensure resources are adequate to meet these needs. |
| Upon approval, signed documents should be forwarded to Accounting at USA.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    |
